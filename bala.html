S.P.BALASUBRAHMANYAM

"S. P. Balasubrahmanyam, often referred to as SPB, was a legendary Indian playback singer, actor, music director, and voice actor. He was born on June 4, 1946, in Nellore, Andhra Pradesh, India, and passed away on September 25, 2020. Balasubrahmanyam's career spanned over five decades, during which he recorded over 40,000 songs in various languages, including Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Hindi, and Malayalam. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest playback singers in the history of Indian cinema. His versatility and unique voice made him a favorite among music directors and composers, and he collaborated with many leading figures in the Indian film industry. He won numerous awards for his contributions to music, including several National Film Awards and Filmfare Awards. Apart from his singing career, Balasubrahmanyam also ventured into acting and voice acting, lending his voice to many iconic characters in Indian cinema. He was known for his humility, dedication to his craft, and his ability to convey deep emotions through his singing. Balasubrahmanyam's legacy continues to live on through his vast body of work, and he remains an inspiration to aspiring singers and musicians across India and beyond".
