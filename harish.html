"Harish Raghavendra is a talented playback singer in the Indian music industry, primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. Here's a brief overview of Harish Raghavendra: 1. **Early Life:** Harish Raghavendra was born on January 7, 1976, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.
